About Me

Crafting code with purpose & precision.

A glimpse into who I am, where I study, and the skills I've built along the way.

I'm Arif Şevki Yılmaz, a Computer Engineering student at Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University, expecting to graduate in 2027. I have a genuine curiosity for how things work — from low-level microprocessor systems to high-level data abstractions.

Throughout my studies, I have built a solid foundation in C/C++, Java, and Python, applying them to diverse challenges. My academic work ranges from developing smart home automation architectures to analyzing air pollution data using signal processing and scientific computing tools like Pandas and NumPy. Today, my greatest passion lies in AI Engineering, where I aim to combine system design skills with artificial intelligence to build intelligent, data-driven solutions.

Beyond the screen, I believe in the importance of stepping back to recharge. When I'm not writing code, you can usually find me traveling to explore new environments, or immersing myself in the rich worlds, stories, and mechanics of single-player PC games.
